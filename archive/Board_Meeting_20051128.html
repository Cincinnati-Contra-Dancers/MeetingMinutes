New Payment Policy for Out-of-Town bands and callers.  After much discussion it was decided:

Mondays only:  Band Members $65 per person up to 3 people (total - $195).  $65 per caller.  A one time traveling expense payment of $30 for those traveling >150 miles.

Saturday s only: Band Members $125 per person up to 4 people (total $500).  $125 per caller.  A one time traveling expense payment of $50 for those traveling > 150 miles.

 

Respectfully submitted by Larry Lankford, secretary, and Eileen Bens.
