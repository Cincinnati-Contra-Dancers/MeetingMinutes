I) Child Admission Policy

Child Admission Policy: All children under 18 admitted free. Passed.

 

II) Email Flyers

John B  (not in attendance) had suggested emailing flyers (instead of "snail mail") to all on our email list and for those not on our email list flyers will be mailed by regular mail. Passed.

 

III) Sound

John McCain recommended purchasing a snake cable and cabinet for storage.

This equipment could be used in any location. Estimates $300-$400 for snake

and $300 for cabinet. In addition, monitors at each microphone to control

sound will be looked into. $900 was approved for purchase of snake and

cabinet.

 

IV) Location

Charley presented the results of the survey to date. Harmony Lodge and St.

Marks are forerunners. About the same number of people attended each test

dance. There was no question on the survey dealing with cost issues.

The big issue with Harmony is that Monday night is not available. New issues

with St. Marks-recently a decision was made to merge St. Marks parish and

St. Cecilia's. The Pastor is not sure if he can commit to us for the long

term. The Vogt's need to follow up on this.

 

A new survey is needed.

Need to gather info on:

Choice between forerunners

Willingness to switch nights, if so, night preferences

Eileen is following up on survey. She has list of halls and info but needs

others to check them out. Survey to be distributed over a 6-8 week period of

time to get best possible response.

 

Additional location issues:

Steve Tossey checking on crime rates in area of both halls.
