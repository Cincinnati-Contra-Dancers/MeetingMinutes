Minutes of Cincinnati Contra Dance (CCD) meeting Monday May 16, 2005

 

Those attending: Christie Brock, John Brockman, Debbie Lucas, Susan Vogt, Eileen Bens, Karen Keeton, Jim Vogt, John McCain, Pam Stoll, Mary Jane Miller, Larry Lankford.

 

Minutes of the 11-22-04 meeting were read and approved.

 

Treasurer Karen Keaton reported that we cleared approximately $1300-1400 on the Pigtown Fling. Not all issues are finalized. John McCain noted that we have about $17K in CD's and about $8K in the checking account. John will arrange to put ($$$) of the checking into another CD.

 

There will be a couples dance workshop from 2PM to 5PM before the Sept. 10th dance.  Advanced Waltz and Bluesy Swing will be taught by Andy and Ruth Ann. Admission will be $6. Footloose (the Sat night band) will provide music. The three band members and Andy and Ruth Ann will each receive $50 for their efforts.

 

We will make announcements before the last contra dance of the first half of the dance to better inform those dancers who must leave early on Monday nights.

 

John McCain will assemble a list of contacts for regional dance groups to facilitate coordination of multiple venues for traveling bands and callers.

 

John Brockman will provide an updated mailing and phone list for the CCD. A preliminary list will be provided for people to verify or correct information before the list is finalized and made available to the membership.

 

John Brockman will talk to Bob Frankenhoff to coordinate further evaluation and investigation for permanently mounting speakers on the walls to make setting up the sound easier. John McCain will ask Debbie Barrett, who works at Cincinnati State to see if the college has anyone they can recommend.

 

For Saturday dances we will start the last waltz at 11:15PM. We will raise the donation from $6 to $7 for Sat night dances starting with the (date?) dance.
